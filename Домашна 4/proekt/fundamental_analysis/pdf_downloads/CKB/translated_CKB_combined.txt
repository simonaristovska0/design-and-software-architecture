According to Chapter 6 of the Co-operative Rules, we report on the following price sensitive information: Central Co-operative Bank AD Skopje reports that a deal has been realised with Central Co-operative Bank ADA Sofia after making the agreement on a sovereign debt of 1 million EUR. Central Co-operative Bank ADA Sofia is the dominant owner of the Central Co-operative Bank AD Skopje with 87.35% participation in the total share of the Bank.
